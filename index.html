Our Philosophy

Machomind Abacus is an organization committed to rendering on Abacus Maths and Whole Brain Development programme which promises to raise best brains students. Our objective is to introduce well researched, need based, quality and skill development programmes for children within the ages of 3 to 13years and beyond.

Machomind Abacus and Brain Development Programme is the result of the hard work of a team of educators who believe that proficiency in Maths is a must for any individual to succeed in life.

Soroban, a Japanese abacus, is best used as a visual aid in helping children comprehend and establish strong Maths skills. Strong basic Maths skills are needed to excel in algebra, geometry, calculus, trigonometry, chemistry, physics, account, economics, geography, nursing, arts, architecture, and many other subjects. Strong Maths knowledge is the backbone for success in any profession.

Being able to do basic Maths mentally boosts a child’s confidence to a whole new level. The benefit of this confidence can also be realized in all other subjects the child tends to learn. Helping children gain confidence and realize that they are geniuses is the goal of our organization and programme.

Pre School

Activity Description

The programme aims to familiarize the preschoolers with the concepts of the abacus mental arithmetic formula in order to do simple addition and subtraction of a series of numbers. Children will develop mathematical thinking from the principles of counting and the development of abstraction and numerical reasoning.

With the programme, children complete pre-school education with solid maths-related learning, particularly with numbers and relationships.

Primary

Activity Description

This programme is open to the primary students from Primary 1 and higher. The programme starts with the basic foundation course in a fun way where in the concept, methodology, usage of abacus and the fingering movements of beads using both hands simultaneously are introduced.

Thereafter, the course progresses to net higher levels. The next higher levels classes will use the formula and abacus skills learned in basic level to project the imaginary abacus in the students' mind. Number will be converted to beads of the abacus in the students’ mind. With practice, student's mental calculation skills develop. In the advanced classes, the abacus is withdrawn from the child ad they are trained to visualize an "Imaginary Abacus" to perform different types of mathematical calculations mentally. By this stage, students will have developed the skills of amazingly fast mental calculations just by looking at the numbers.

Post Primary

Activity Description

This is specially designed for teens, young adults andmatured learners who missed out the opportunities to learn the mentalarithmetic skill in early years but are interested in uncovering the myths of abacus and at the same time, in acquiring the great benefits the abacus can offer. It is never too late for one to acquire these skills so as to improve one's memory, concentration and mental power.
